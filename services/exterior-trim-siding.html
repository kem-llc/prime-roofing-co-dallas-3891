Exterior Trim & Siding

Professional installation and repair of trim, fascia, and siding.

Schedule this service

Overview

Enhance your home's curb appeal and protection with our exterior trim and siding services. We install and repair fascia, soffits, crown molding, and siding using durable materials like wood, vinyl, and fiber cement. Our attention to detail ensures clean lines and a polished look that stands up to Texas weather. Whether you're updating a single section or re-siding your entire home, we deliver quality results.

What's included

  • Boosts curb appeal and home value
  • Protects against moisture and pests
  • Energy-efficient insulation options
  • Low-maintenance, long-lasting materials

Common questions

How often should siding be replaced?

Wood siding lasts 20-40 years, vinyl 20-30 years, and fiber cement 50+ years with proper maintenance.

Do you repair damaged trim?

Yes, we repair or replace rotted or damaged trim, fascia, and soffits to restore your home's exterior.
