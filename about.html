Our story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

To provide Dallas homeowners with exceptional carpentry and roofing services that combine quality materials, skilled workmanship, and outstanding customer care.

Prime Roofing Co was founded in 1990 by master carpenter John Prime, who believed that every home deserves attention to detail. Starting with a small crew and a commitment to excellence, John built a reputation for reliable, high-quality work. Over the decades, we've grown into a full-service carpentry and roofing company serving the Dallas area.

Today, our team continues John's legacy by treating every project as a reflection of our values: integrity, craftsmanship, and respect for your home. Whether we're building a custom deck or repairing a roof, we approach each job with the same dedication that has earned us the trust of hundreds of homeowners.
