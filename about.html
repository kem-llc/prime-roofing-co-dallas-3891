Our story

Crafting Quality Since 2008

Our mission is to provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with exceptional carpentry services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. We are committed to safety, integrity, and complete customer satisfaction.

Prime Roofing Co was founded in 2008 by master carpenter John Miller, who saw a need for reliable, high-quality carpentry in the Dallas area. Starting with a small crew and a focus on roofing and framing, the company quickly earned a reputation for precision and professionalism. Over the years, we expanded our services to include custom decks, cabinetry, and exterior trim, always maintaining the same dedication to excellence.

Today, Prime Roofing Co is a trusted name in Dallas construction. Our team of skilled carpenters brings decades of combined experience to every project. We take pride in our work, using only the best materials and techniques to ensure lasting results. Whether it's a small repair or a major renovation, we treat every home like our own.
